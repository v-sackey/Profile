Thought Pieces

When people ask me, "Veronica, what exactly do you write about?" I pause.
I write out my thoughts, and yours. As varying as thoughts can be. One minute, you're thinking of getting an apple, the next, you're thinking, "Oh, how?? I haven't danced Salsa in a while!" You see, variant. However, after 4 or 5 varying thoughts, you line them up, and that's a story! This is what I do: I make stories out of disassociated thoughts. And the beauty? I don't know what the story is about until I am done!
